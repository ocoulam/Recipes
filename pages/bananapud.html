Banana Pudding Recipe

Look at this delicious banana pudding! After you follow these directions, you'll be able to make some too.

Ingredients:

  • Mix and pudding mix
  • Sweetened condensed milk
  • Vanilla
  • Frozen whipped topping, like Cool Whip
  • Vanilla wafers
  • Bananas

Directions:

  1. Whisk the budding mix into the milk
  2. Add the sweetened condensed milk and vanilla
  3. Fold in the frozen whipped topping
  4. Layer the mixture with the sliced bananas and vanilla wafers
  5. You're all done! Enjoy your delicious banana pudding
